Question: What does file.readline do? 
Answer: Reads the first line in a file. 
Use: Eyeballing a text file for content, e.g. loop through and print the first five lines to get an idea of what it is. 

Question: What does string.strip do? 
Answer: Removes both leading and trailing characters from a string. 
Use: Would be useful for determining if something is palindromic.
